## Why

As we prepare the application for final deployment and presentation to evaluators, the root route (`/`) must serve as a high-converting, visually striking SaaS landing page. It needs to act as a storefront that immediately communicates value and serves as visual documentation for our unique "Alive AI" capabilities.

## What Changes

- **Overhaul Root Page**: Replace the default Next.js boilerplate in `app/page.jsx` with a premium dark-themed landing page.
- **Hero Section**: Add a massive headline, subtitle emphasizing AI productivity, and a glowing CTA button linking to `/dashboard`.
- **Features Grid**: Create three glassmorphism cards detailing core features: "Sistema Nervoso (Linter)", "Kanban Preditivo", and "Pomodoro Dinâmico".
- **Pro Plan Banner**: Add a wide gradient banner at the bottom teasing the "Plano Pro: Consultoria de Dados" to demonstrate commercial viability.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `premium-landing-page`: A responsive, premium marketing storefront highlighting the "Alive AI" features of EduTrack-Ai.

## Impact

- **Affected code**: `app/page.jsx`
- **User Experience**: Drastically improves the first impression for new visitors and evaluators, clearly articulating the project's value proposition before they log in.
